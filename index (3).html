Introduction

A Life Devoted to Preserving
the Himalayan Literary Heritage

Khenpo Kunga Rinchen is a distinguished Khenpo of the Sakya tradition—a scholar, editor, teacher, and now a writer—whose life's work stands as a testament to the power of devotion in the service of knowledge. Across nearly a decade of painstaking labour, he undertook what may be one of the most significant textual preservation projects in modern Tibetan Buddhist history: the compilation and publication of the Gyud-de Kuntu, the definitive Collection of Tantric Explanations.

His journey bridges centuries and continents—from the fragile, hand-inscribed folios of medieval Himalayan monasteries to the pages of contemporary fiction workshops in the American Midwest—embodying a rare synthesis of ancient wisdom and modern creative expression.

The Great Work

The Gyud-de Kuntu

For nine unbroken years, Khenpo Kunga Rinchen devoted himself to the monumental task of compiling the Gyud-de Kuntu—a vast collection of tantric explanatory texts preserved in ancient, handwritten manuscripts spanning 700 to 1,000 years. Many of these fragile folios, penned in archaic scripts and marked by orthographic variations lost to modern readers, stood on the very threshold of illegibility.

With extraordinary patience and philological rigour, he deciphered, collated, and edited these texts—rescuing an irreplaceable body of sacred knowledge from the slow erasure of time. The result is a landmark 116-volume published collection: a definitive treasury of the Sakya tantric literary tradition, now secured for generations of scholars and practitioners to come.

Sacred Lineage

Guided by Living Masters

This monumental work was carried out under the blessings and guidance of two of the most eminent masters of the Sakya tradition—figures whose authority and scholarship lent the project both its spiritual legitimacy and its scholarly direction.

H.E. Luding Khenchen Rinpoche
One of the most revered holders of the Sakya lineage, whose guidance ensured the spiritual integrity and doctrinal fidelity of the collection throughout its compilation.
H.E. Khenchen Appey Rinpoche
A towering scholar and founding figure of modern Sakya academic study, whose support provided the philological and editorial foundations upon which the project rests.
H.E. Dzongsar Khyentse Rinpoche
A visionary teacher, filmmaker, and patron of the arts, whose support enabled Khenpo Kunga Rinchen's creative transition into the world of contemporary fiction writing.

The Great Compilation

Creative Horizons

With the support of H.E. Dzongsar Khyentse Rinpoche, he is currently pursuing a Master of Fine Arts in Creative Writing (Fiction) at the University of Missouri–Kansas City—bringing the storytelling instincts of an ancient tradition into dialogue with modern literary craft.
