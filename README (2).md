# ☸ Khenpo Kunga Rinchen

**Guardian of the Sacred Word — Bridging Ancient Wisdom and Contemporary Storytelling**

---

Khenpo Kunga Rinchen is a distinguished Khenpo of the Sakya tradition, recognized for his extraordinary dedication to preserving the Himalayan literary heritage. Over nine years, he compiled the **Gyud-de Kuntu** (རྒྱུད་བཤད་ཀུན་བཏུས།) — a landmark **116-volume** collection of tantric explanatory texts, rescuing handwritten manuscripts spanning 700 to 1,000 years from the edge of illegibility.

This monumental work was carried out under the guidance and support of **H.E. Luding Khenchen Rinpoche** and **H.E. Khenchen Appey Rinpoche**.

After serving as Khenpo at the **Pal Sakya Tantric College**, he expanded into creative writing with the support of **H.E. Dzongsar Khyentse Rinpoche**, and is currently pursuing an **MFA in Creative Writing (Fiction)** at the University of Missouri–Kansas City.

Today, he continues to teach the Dharma globally while shaping new narratives that carry the depth of an ancient tradition into the modern literary world.
